        Labels and scores

        Likelihood score (0–100%) is how much this resident’s program activity—education records, health check-ins, counseling sessions, home visits, plus safehouse, case category, and referral—looks like residents who were later marked on a positive path in the case file. It is a similarity ranking from patterns in past data, not a promise about the future, not a clinical or legal judgment, and not a substitute for staff decisions.

        Positive path in the last column matches the notebook: risk improved compared with intake and/or reintegration marked in progress or completed. The score deliberately does not use today’s risk level as an input, so it does not double-count what is already in the case record.

        For staff who want the numbers: the notebook holds out residents for a fair check and reports how well the score lines up with the case label and with risk level. Open the notebook export for the full table.
