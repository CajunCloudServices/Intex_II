## Replacement Rules

- keep assets in `.webp` when possible
- prefer wide landscape compositions for the current layout
- optimize images before committing so they stay in a web-friendly size range
- do not overwrite an asset casually; replace it intentionally where the page composition is known

## Brand Direction

The current visual direction is:

- calm
- trustworthy
- nonprofit-professional
- warm rather than corporate
- privacy-conscious and dignity-first

When adding new imagery, stay away from exploitative or sensational “survivor” visuals. Prefer spaces, resources, and atmosphere over identifiable vulnerable people.
